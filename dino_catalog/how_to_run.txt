# to print all facts of a given dino
ruby run_dinodex.rb name:Giganotosaurus
Dinosaurs with ["name:Giganotosaurus"]
Giganotosaurus is a Biped from Late Cretaceous period and South America continent with Carnivore diet and 30420lbs weight. Largest hunter and also the coolest ever.
